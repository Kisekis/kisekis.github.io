Proper cones 真锥

is a proper cone if

  • K is convex
  • K is closed
  • K is solid
  • K is pointed（contains no line ）

Cone:从原点出发的k条射线的集合

Convex cone：包含最外侧两条射线间的所有点

Proper cone：只有一个方向，不关于原点对称

A proper cone K can be used to define a generalized inequality
