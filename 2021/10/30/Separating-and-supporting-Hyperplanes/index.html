Converse separating hyperplane theorems

Suppose C and D are convex sets，with C open, and there exists an affine function f that is nonpositive on C

and nonnegative on D. Then C and D are disjoint.

如果不加 open 的限定条件，可以是相切的两个convex set

Any two convex sets C and D, at least one of which is open, are disjoint if and only if there exists a separating hyperplane.
